About Us

NEROL Company was established in 1979 in Cairo, Egypt. Initially, its activities were confined to manufacturing and distributing detergents. Later on in 1989 NEROL expanded its activities by providing a wide range of chemical raw materials needed in Egyptian industries; including personal care ,cosmetics, home care ,detergents, painting ,coating, textile, oil and drilling industries.
